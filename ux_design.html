Web Applications

The Spoke

I'm leading the UX design for The Spoke, an online platorm that allows users to create, search and store lifestyle recommendations (restaurants, movies, books, etc).

Users, friends, and publishers can view and share recommendations through smart lists. The Spoke also connects to services including Netflix, Yelp, Foursquare and Seamless, providing descriptions, ratings, reviews, and availablility on a single page to allow users to make a decision and take action directly on the platform.

Challenges include:

  • Creating a cohesive interface that works with diverse verticles (restaurants, books)
  • Creating subtle onboarding
  • Optimizing both extensive functionality and a clean, intuative interface

Scheduling App

I designed an internal scheduling app for a company that places substitute teachers in Charter Schools.

The company was expanding rapidly and needed to find a way to automate aspects of their scheduling process, while still maintaining a "human" feel.

Challenges included:

  • Identifying the most commmon errors and time-consuming aspects of the current process
  • Designing within the existing process and technology constraints
  • Understanding company external communication standards to determine what should and shouldn't be automated
  • Maintaining the company's brand identity
